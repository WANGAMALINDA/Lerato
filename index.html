Our Story

Excellence in
Every Detail

Lerato The Cooking Cyclist is a professional catering and event solutions brand delivering high-quality culinary experiences tailored for corporate and private clients. Built on the powerful tagline "We serve an experience", we combine exceptional cuisine, efficient service delivery, and refined event execution.

Founded by Lerato — a passionate cyclist, chef, and entrepreneur — the brand embodies creativity, resilience, excellence, and movement, brought to life through the distinctive 'The Cooking Cyclist' concept.

We pride ourselves in delivering consistent, reliable, and high-standard catering solutions for a wide range of corporate functions.
